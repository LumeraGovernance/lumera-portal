Republic of Lumera

Republic of Lumera is an e-nation designed to connect people across the globe through a shared Afro-European identity — built on the internet, governed by its citizens, and shaped by a bold vision for what civilisation can become.

What is an e-nation

An e-nation is a community that organises itself around a shared digital identity and governance structure. Members hold citizenship not through geography, but through participation — contributing to a living, evolving state that exists wherever its people do.

What makes Lumera special

Lumeran society refuses the mundane. Rather than accepting the flatness of the modern world, Lumera dares to engineer bold, unthinkably creative new ways of being human — a civilisation where culture, economy, and language are reinvented from first principles.

Lumera Projects

A portfolio of transformative initiatives, each designed to make Lumera a self-sustaining and forward-looking society:

  • Lumera City — A futuristic city comprised of Anolles, designed for sustainability and innovation.
  • Lumera Marketplace — A marketplace that cuts out the middleman to provide a self-sufficient society.
  • Lumera Education — High quality, accessible, fair education for all citizens.
  • Lumera Economic System — An energy-based, non-consumeristic capitalist economy.
  • Lumeran Language — An adapted version of Ithkuil, crafted for clarity and cultural expression.
