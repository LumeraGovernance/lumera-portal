Become a Citizen of Lumera

Citizenship is voluntary and entered by Conscientious Consent from age 16.

Step 1 — Apply

Complete the application form. You will choose your Lumerian name and agree to the Oath.

Open Application Form →

Step 2 — Oath of Conscientious Consent

Attend a live oath ceremony (Discord) or submit a recorded oath. Two citizens will witness.

Step 3 — Certificate & Registry

Upon approval, you receive a certificate and your Registry ID (e.g., LUM0123). A notice is published in the Gazette.
